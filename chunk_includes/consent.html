Consent Form

Section 1: Introduction

We are researchers in the Department of English at Morehead State University.

Section 2: Description of the study and study procedures

We are conducting a research study to better understand human language.

The name of the study is Accents and Syntactic Attachment 11. The IRB Project is 16-04-82R1. The person in charge of the study is Dr. Katy Carlson.

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to provide examples of language expressions and give judgments about the acceptability and/or interpretation of language expressions.

Section 3: Risks

Your participation in this study does not involve any physical or emotional risk to you beyond that of everyday life.

Section 4: Benefits

You are not likely to have any direct benefit from being in this research study.

Section 5: Alternatives

You may choose to not participate in this research study.

Section 6: Financial Information

You will be paid $6 dollars.

Section 7: Confidentiality

Unless required by law, only the study investigator, members of the investigator's staff, representatives of the National Institutes of Health and the Morehead State University Institutional Review Board will have authority to review your survey records. They are required to maintain confidentiality regarding your identity.

Results of this survey may be used for teaching, research, publications, or presentations at scientific meetings. If your individual results are discussed, your identity will be protected by using a study code number rather than your name or other identifying information.

This survey is being hosted by Amazon Mechanical Turk and involves a secure connection. Amazon Mechanical Turk retains a copy of your study data. Terms of Service, addressing confidentiality, may be viewed at https://www.mturk.com/mturk/conditionsofuse. Amazon Mechanical Turk protects the security of your information by using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts the information. This information is stored encrypted in a database behind multiple firewalls.

Section 8: Subject's Rights

Your participation in this research study is completely voluntary. You can withdraw at any time. Choosing not to be in this study or to stop being in this study will not result in any penalty to you or loss of benefit to which you are entitled.
